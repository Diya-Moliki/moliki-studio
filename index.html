From sketch to finished room, in four moves.

A fixed sequence we follow on every project, so nothing about your space is left to guesswork.

I. Discover

Walkthrough & brief

II. Draft

Plans & mood boards

III. Source

Procurement & trades

Styling & reveal

Final placement, styling, and a walkthrough — the room handed back to you, finished.

Principal Designer

Hi, I'm the studio behind Moliki — and every project starts at your floor plan, not a mood board.

I trained in spatial design before moving into residential and boutique commercial interiors, which means every project starts with how a room is actually used — traffic flow, light, storage — before a single fabric swatch enters the conversation.

The result is interiors that photograph well, but more importantly, hold up to daily life: kids, dinner parties, late nights at the kitchen island, all of it.
